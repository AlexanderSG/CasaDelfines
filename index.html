Casa de los Delfines is a Hostel & surf Academy designed for beach and water lovers

About us

The hostel has a friendly staff that creates the perfect atmosphere for your stay. Here you can enjoy the natural environmeint, relax at the hammack placed in the garden, enjoy the ocean view or just relax in the pool. The Hostel can also arrange transportation to all of the beaches in the area, including surf lessons.

The back terrace allows for great evening parties with large dry bar, seating for four at the bar, and seating for twelve or more. Equipment includes wifi for the entire property (security protected), a 18kw Kohler generator with low hours and a 500 lb tank, ceiling fans on the terrace, barbeque grill (gas), beer cooler, among others.
